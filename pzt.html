Method 2: REM vs. PX Element Measurement

Method Description: This method creates two hidden elements: one with a fixed width in pixels (e.g., 16px) and another with a width in rem units (e.g., 1rem). By comparing their actual rendered widths, we can infer the text zoom level, as rem units are relative to the root font size, which is affected by text zoom.
